About PAT

The company PAT , TUNISIAN ANIMAL PROTECTION ASSOCIATION , is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the welfare and rights of animals in Tunisia. Founded in 2015, our association was born out of a deep passion for animals and a strong commitment to making a difference in their lives. We recognized the growing need for assistance in the realm of animal welfare and decided to take action. Since then, we have been working tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and find loving homes for animals in need .
